Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

1. Information We Collect

Power Ball Arena 3D is designed to respect your privacy. We collect minimal information necessary for game functionality:

  • Game progress and high scores (stored locally on your device)
  • Audio and graphics preferences (stored locally)
  • Anonymous usage analytics to improve game performance

2. How We Use Information

The information we collect is used to:

  • Provide and maintain game functionality
  • Save your game preferences and progress
  • Improve game performance and user experience
  • Display relevant advertisements (when applicable)

3. Local Storage

Game data is primarily stored locally on your device using browser local storage. This includes:

  • Game settings and preferences
  • Audio volume settings
  • Tutorial completion status
  • Speed booster counts

4. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services for:

  • Analytics to understand game usage patterns
  • Advertising to support free gameplay
  • Content delivery for optimal performance

5. Cookies and Tracking

We use minimal tracking technologies to:

  • Remember your game preferences
  • Provide personalized advertising experiences
  • Analyze game usage for improvements

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information. However, no internet transmission is 100% secure.

7. Children's Privacy

Our game is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent.

8. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

9. Contact Information

For privacy-related questions or concerns, please contact us through the game's feedback system.

This privacy policy ensures compliance with advertising networks and data protection regulations.
